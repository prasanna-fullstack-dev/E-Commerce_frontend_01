fusion of tradition, technology & innovation

At Saptham TAILA a team of industry experts constantly strive to ensure hygienic, nutrient rich and best in class edible and essential oils are produced. TAILA edible oils are extracted at room temperature using the ade old conventional Gaana/Chekku/Ghani extractor (Wooden/Cast Iron Rotary)- a combination of tradition, technology and innovation.

Manohar lyer, the founder CEO is entrepreneur and a former IT Professional with over 22 years of experience across multiple industries.

TAILA is an exclusive edible and essential oil brand of Saptham Food and Beverages.
